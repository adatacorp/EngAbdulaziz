My Services

Geotechnical Survey

I perform detailed geotechnical surveys to assess soil and subsurface conditions essential for safe and sustainable designs. My work involves rigorous field investigations and precise data analysis to inform foundation and structural planning. I use state-of-the-art modeling techniques to minimize risks and optimize site performance.

I conduct comprehensive audits to evaluate the safety and performance of existing structures.
